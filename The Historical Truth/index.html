What is the Historical Truth?

The Historical Truth is a website and browser extension that will help you to find the truth behind the information you see. We look at hundreds of articles everyday, but how do we know that what we are reading is the truth? The Historical Truth helps you to find the biased from your sources and what information you can trust, so that you can find the Historical Truth.
